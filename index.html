Projects

Improving the accuracy of collective prediction by maintaining the diversity of opinions
Background: Decisions made by a single individual tend to be inaccurate compared to decisionsmade by majority. Collective intelligence is a process of sharing or group intelligence, and assembling opinions from others into decision-making
Add Incentive: Based on previous research to give incentive for agents and induce collective intelligence, in order to improve the prediction accuracy.
Evaluation: Different incentives can be used to dynamically improve the prediction accuracy with different number of agents participate in.
Data-based traffic route planning in Japan
Data Processing: Collect and process data to obtain correlations between on geographic information data and commuting time among Japanese cities.
Data Analysis and Visualization: Analyze the traffic connections between cities (i.e. the means of transportation used, the time, the number of nodes passing through) to obtain the geographic transportation network graph between cities in Japan.
Traffic Optimization: Find hot spot cities through data visualization,and let them as radiation centers to add faster or new traffic routes, in order to reduce the commuting time of public equipment between cities.
